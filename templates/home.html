Usage

Please do

    GET /extension

Options

You may provide ?default=unknown parameter for me to render it if I've no idea what your extension is.

You may provide Accept: mime/type header to choose renderer:

  • text/plain
  • text/html
  • application/json

Examples

  • $ curl $HOST/txt -H 'Accept: application/json'
    {"extension": "txt", "mime": "text/plain"}
  • $ curl $HOST/42?default=no-match -H 'Accept: text/plain'
    no-match
